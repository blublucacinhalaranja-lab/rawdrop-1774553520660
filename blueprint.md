# **App Name**: Clean Canvas

## Core Features:

- Vazio: Criação de pastas base e vazias para IA, como 'ia.js', sem integração com serviços do Google.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color is a subtle, cool grey (#575E66), conveying a sense of calm and professionalism, perfect for a starting point.
- The background color is a very light grey (#F3F5F7), providing a clean, bright foundation that is easy on the eyes.
- An accent color of light cyan (#4DCEE0) provides a fresh, modern touch for subtle highlights or interactive elements, offering a gentle visual contrast without being distracting.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its clean, modern, and highly legible characteristics, providing an objective and versatile base for any content.
- Utilize minimalistic, outline-style icons only if absolutely necessary, maintaining the app's clean and 'empty' aesthetic.
- A spacious, airy layout with ample whitespace is preferred, ensuring maximum flexibility and a sense of openness on the canvas.
- Animations should be non-existent or extremely subtle and brief, limited to basic interaction feedback, in line with the minimal nature of the application.
